## sum_cost_basis property


Gets or sets the cost basis. This is the total cost basis for all transactions reported in this summary.For Form 8949 Code A and D transactions, this
should be the cost basis as reported to the IRS.For all other transactions, the cost basis should be the cost basis as known by the provider.
